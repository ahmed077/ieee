Our Committees

  1. Public Relations

    • Job Description

      Public Relations committee is considered as a main mirror of the IEEE PUA SB which reflexes the community status and activities internally and externally. PR is not only our mirror, image or even our voice, PR is about our story and the way we tell it.

    • Responsibilities

      • Managing our reputation by Developing ideas, stories, and tactics to help deliver our message to people.
      • Getting sponsors & partners and help Treasure to make deals with them.
      • Getting speakers and deal with them and represent them in Events.
      • Making a wide network of Speakers, VIPs & foundations.
      • Represent IEEE PUA SB at the formal events (local & international).
      • Making proposal when IEEE PUA deal with any administration.

    • Criteria

      • Basic Knowledge in Public relation.
      • Good public speaker & communicator.
      • Good range of relations and contacts.
      • Negotiation skills.
      • Judgment and Making decision
      • Leadership and motivation.
      • Creativity and innovation.
      • Get straight to the point.
      • Ability to gain the listener's attention.

      "Since we can’t change reality, let us change the eyes which see reality" - Nikos Kazantzakis

  2. Human Resources

    • Job Description

      Maintains the work structure by updating job requirements .Tracking the performance and the participation of each member of the team and announcing it when needed. Scheduling management meetings with the members.

    • Responsibilities

      • Recruiting.
      • Training and evaluating members.
      • Monitoring the members and the work’s progress.
      • Research and development.
      • Crisis management.
      • Putting fines and awards systems.
      • Planning.

    • Criteria

      • Communicator and presenter.
      • Innovation and creativity.
      • Approachable.
      • Time management and Self discipline.
      • Delegator and decision maker.
      • Business Acumen.
      • Trust advisor.
      • Motivator and leader.

      "nothing worth having comes easy"

  3. Logistics & Organization Committee

      This committee has two basic departments, they are Logistics and Organization

    • Organization

    • Responsibilities

      • Deal with suppliers to provide all needed materials and products with low prices and high quality for our events and for the other committees and create database of suppliers.
      • Print posters, banners, brochures, pins and all other materials the team needs.
      • It coordinates with the treasure for the financial issues for getting the lowest prices for what we need in the events.

    • Logistics.

    • Responsibilities

      • It’s responsible for organizing the event.
      • Get the hall prepared for evacuation if something wrong happened.
      • Prepare the hall for the host. (How much chairs does He/ She need, the VIP launch, where is the registration desk ….Etc.).
      • Putting a calendar for every event after coordinating with PR committee (the timing for every session).
      • Organizing the registration for the attendees with the registration committee and designing and building the booth and info desks for every event.

    • Criteria

      • Good teamwork player.
      • Having the ability to multi-task in fast paced dynamic environment.
      • Having a good Communication skills.
      • Creative thinking.
      • Reliability.
      • Flexible.
      • High Commitment.
      • Having a good Crisis management & Problem Solving skills.

      "Stop at nothing…achieve everything " - Gary Whitehill

    • Job Description

      We represent the branch in front of the attendees; we are the main connection between IEEE and attendees.

    • Responsibilities

      • Create the “Form” and “feedback” for events.

  5. Media

    • Job Description

      This committee is the branch’s representative on the different social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc...). The committee’s role is marketing the branch, managing the branch’s relationships with its followers and publish its latest news to them on our social media accounts like the events, the awards, activities, etc.…, It also monitors the progress in the followers’ engagement with the branch’s activities on the social media and their feedback.

    • Responsibilities

      • Responsible for announcing, spreading and advertising for any upcoming events, Projects, sessions or workshops.
      • Manage all Pages and accounts at all social media channels.
      • Increasing the interaction and widening our knowledge base through all possible social media means.
      • Knowing audience needs through online surveys and questionnaires.

    • Criteria

      • Has a basic knowledge in marketing. And an interest in further knowledge in it.
      • Has a good writing language.
      • A fast learner.
      • A good communicator. (For marketing purposes).
      • Organized.
      • Good at team work..

      "Don't build link. Build relationships"

  6. Graphics

    • Job Description

      Graphics committee is a minor committee listed under major media committee, it's the committee which is responsible for providing media products like posters, banners, covers…etc. Also it works with other media committees as the photography and social media.

    • Responsibilities

      • Design Posters, covers, banners…etc. in the available time.
      • Working with other committees like the photography committee for providing the footers and layouts of events' photos.
      • Design and Update logos
      • Provide suitable designs and photos for the social media committee.
      • Design fliers and certificates when needed.
      • Make creative motivational videos when needed.

    • Criteria

      • Creative and innovative (if not, the volunteer try to work hard for improving himself by reviewing and browsing).
      • Having a good background in video making using “Adobe After Effects” or any similar program.
      • Respect monetary and other opinions.
      • Great background in Photoshop tools and use.
      • Working with other programs like light room, illustrator, after effect...etc. giving him the priority.
      • Good time manager.
      • Patient.

      "Graphic designer is the artist who can translate and express an idea or imagination to real, He is the MIND READER" - Nadeem Usama

  7. Photography

    • Job Description

      Photographers use their equipment to record the events.

    • Responsibilities

      • Setting up photographic equipment.
      • Taking pictures.
      • Editing and retouching images.

      • Recording videos before the events and create proclamation for the events.

      • Creative with a good eye for a picture.
